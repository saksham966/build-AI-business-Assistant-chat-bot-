AI Business Assistant Chatbot & Image Analyzer

An intelligent multimodal AI assistant powered by Groq LLMs for business insights, conversational support(until you write quiet or Goodbye), and image analysis.

🚀 Overview

AI Business Assistant & Image Analyzer is a powerful AI-driven application that combines:

🤖 Business Assistant Chatbot
Answers business-related queries
Provides market insights and recommendations
Assists with decision-making and business planning
Supports natural language conversations
🖼️ Image Analyzer Chatbot
Analyzes uploaded images using AI vision capabilities
Generates detailed descriptions of images
Identifies objects, scenes, and visual elements
Answers user questions about uploaded images

The project leverages Groq's ultra-fast inference engine to deliver real-time responses with low latency and high performance.
